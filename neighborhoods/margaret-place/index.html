About Margaret Place

Margaret Place is one of Lake Charles' most distinguished historic neighborhoods, developed in the early 1900s. The neighborhood is listed on the National Register of Historic Places and features stunning Craftsman, Colonial Revival, and Tudor-style homes. Many homes feature original architectural details including high ceilings, large windows, and unique floor plans.

The neighborhood's mature oak trees and walkable streets make it one of the most desirable areas in Lake Charles. Many homeowners in Margaret Place have invested in updating their HVAC systems while preserving their home's historic character.
