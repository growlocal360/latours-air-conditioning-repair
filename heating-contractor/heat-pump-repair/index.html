Heat Pump Repair in Lake Charles, LA

Expert diagnosis and repair for all heat pump brands. Same-day service available, upfront pricing, and 24/7 emergency repairs.

Is your heat pump not heating or cooling properly? Latour's HVAC provides fast, reliable heat pump repair for Lake Charles homeowners. Our NATE-certified technicians diagnose problems accurately and fix them right the first time—with most repairs completed the same day.

Heat pumps are complex systems that require specialized knowledge to repair properly. Unlike standard air conditioners, heat pumps include a reversing valve and defrost controls that require expert diagnosis. Our technicians are trained specifically on heat pump systems and have the experience to get yours working again quickly.

Common Heat Pump Problems We Repair

  • Heat Pump Not Heating: Could be a stuck reversing valve, low refrigerant, defrost issues, or thermostat problems
  • Heat Pump Not Cooling: Similar causes as cooling issues—refrigerant, airflow, or electrical problems
  • Heat Pump Running Constantly: May indicate low refrigerant, dirty coils, or incorrect sizing
  • Heat Pump Short Cycling: Turning on and off frequently often signals electrical issues or refrigerant problems
  • Ice Building Up: Defrost cycle problems, low refrigerant, or airflow restrictions
  • Heat Pump Making Noise: Grinding, squealing, or rattling indicate mechanical issues
  • Heat Pump Blowing Cold Air in Heat Mode: Reversing valve, refrigerant, or defrost issues
  • High Energy Bills: Efficiency problems often caused by refrigerant leaks or dirty components

Heat Pump Repair vs. Replacement

We provide honest recommendations based on your specific situation:

  • Repair: Usually the best choice for systems under 10 years old with minor issues
  • Consider Replacement: If repair costs exceed 50% of a new system's cost
  • Replace: For units over 15 years old or those needing frequent major repairs

We'll explain all your options and never pressure you into an unnecessary replacement.
