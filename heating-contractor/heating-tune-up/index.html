Heating Tune-Up in Lake Charles, LA

Professional furnace and heat pump maintenance to ensure reliable heating, improve efficiency, and prevent costly winter breakdowns.

A heating tune-up before winter arrives ensures your furnace or heat pump will work reliably when temperatures drop. Latour's HVAC provides comprehensive heating maintenance that improves efficiency, catches problems early, and extends equipment life.

While Lake Charles winters are mild, cold fronts can bring temperatures into the 30s. When that happens, you want to know your heating system is ready. Annual maintenance is the best way to prevent unexpected breakdowns during the coldest nights of the year.

What's Included in a Heating Tune-Up

For Gas Furnaces:

  • Inspect heat exchanger for cracks (safety critical)
  • Check and adjust burner operation
  • Test ignition system
  • Inspect and clean flame sensor
  • Check gas pressure and connections
  • Inspect flue and venting system
  • Test safety controls and limit switches
  • Check and tighten electrical connections
  • Lubricate moving parts
  • Replace or clean air filter
  • Test thermostat operation
  • Check blower motor and airflow
  • Test carbon monoxide levels

For Heat Pumps:

  • Check refrigerant levels
  • Test defrost cycle operation
  • Inspect and clean outdoor coil
  • Check reversing valve operation
  • Test auxiliary/emergency heat
  • Inspect electrical connections
  • Check capacitors and contactors
  • Lubricate moving parts
  • Clean or replace air filter
  • Test thermostat calibration
  • Check airflow and ductwork

Benefits of Annual Heating Maintenance

  • Prevent Breakdowns: Most heating failures are preventable with regular maintenance
  • Safety: Furnace inspections catch dangerous issues like cracked heat exchangers and gas leaks
  • Lower Energy Bills: A tuned system operates 10-25% more efficiently
  • Extended Equipment Life: Proper maintenance can add years to your system's lifespan
  • Maintain Warranty: Many manufacturers require annual maintenance for warranty coverage
  • Peace of Mind: Know your heating will work when you need it

When to Schedule Your Heating Tune-Up

The best time for heating maintenance in Lake Charles is:

  • September-October: Before the heating season begins
  • After Summer: If you have a heat pump, get it checked after heavy summer cooling use

Scheduling before winter ensures any needed repairs can be completed before cold weather arrives.
