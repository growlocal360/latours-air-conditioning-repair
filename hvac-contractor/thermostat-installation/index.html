Thermostat Installation in Lake Charles, LA

Upgrade to a smart or programmable thermostat for better comfort control and energy savings. Professional installation ensures proper setup and compatibility.

A smart thermostat is one of the easiest and most cost-effective upgrades you can make to your HVAC system. Control your home's temperature from anywhere, save 10-23% on energy costs, and enjoy perfect comfort automatically. Latour's HVAC provides professional thermostat installation to ensure compatibility and proper setup.

Today's thermostats do far more than adjust temperature. They learn your schedule, detect when you're away, integrate with other smart home devices, and provide detailed energy reports. Professional installation ensures your new thermostat works correctly with your HVAC system.

Types of Thermostats We Install

Smart Thermostats

The most advanced option, smart thermostats connect to your home Wi-Fi and offer features like:

  • Smartphone control from anywhere
  • Learning algorithms that adapt to your schedule
  • Occupancy sensing to save energy when you're away
  • Energy usage reports and tips
  • Voice control via Alexa, Google, or Siri
  • Integration with other smart home devices

Programmable Thermostats

Set schedules for different times of day and days of the week. More affordable than smart thermostats while still offering significant energy savings.

Basic Digital Thermostats

Simple digital readout with basic controls. A good choice if you prefer simplicity but want accuracy.

Smart Thermostat Brands We Install

  • Google Nest: Learning thermostat that programs itself based on your habits
  • Ecobee: Includes room sensors for balanced whole-home comfort
  • Honeywell Home: Reliable smart thermostats with excellent app control
  • Carrier Infinity: Premium option for Carrier HVAC systems
  • Trane XL: Designed for Trane systems
  • Emerson Sensi: Budget-friendly smart option

Benefits of Smart Thermostats

  • Energy Savings: Typical savings of 10-23% on heating and cooling costs
  • Remote Control: Adjust temperature from anywhere via smartphone
  • Learning Capability: Automatically adjusts to your schedule and preferences
  • Geofencing: Detects when you leave or arrive home and adjusts accordingly
  • Energy Reports: See how much energy you're using and get tips to save more
  • Alerts: Get notified of extreme temperatures, filter reminders, and more
  • Voice Control: Works with Alexa, Google Assistant, and Apple HomeKit

Why Professional Installation?

While many smart thermostats are marketed as DIY-friendly, professional installation offers advantages:

  • Compatibility Check: We verify your HVAC system is compatible before you buy
  • Proper Wiring: We ensure correct connections, including C-wire installation if needed
  • System Configuration: We configure settings specific to your HVAC equipment
  • Testing: We verify proper operation in all modes
  • Tutorial: We show you how to use all the features
  • No Risk: If anything goes wrong, we fix it

C-Wire Installation

Many smart thermostats require a "C-wire" (common wire) for power. Older homes often don't have this wire. If your home lacks a C-wire, we can install one or use an adapter to ensure your smart thermostat works properly.
