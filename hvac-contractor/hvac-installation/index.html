HVAC Installation in Lake Charles, LA

Complete heating and cooling system installation. Professional design, quality equipment from top brands, and expert installation backed by warranties.

Installing a new HVAC system is one of the most important investments you'll make in your home. Latour's HVAC provides complete HVAC installation in Lake Charles—from initial consultation through final testing—ensuring you get the right system, properly sized and expertly installed for years of reliable comfort.

A new HVAC system is more than just equipment. It's the ductwork that delivers conditioned air, the thermostat that controls it, and the installation quality that determines how well it all works together. We take a whole-system approach to ensure every component works in harmony.

Our HVAC Installation Process

  1. Free In-Home Consultation: We evaluate your home, discuss your comfort needs, and review your existing system
  2. Load Calculation: Manual J calculations determine the exact heating and cooling capacity your home needs
  3. System Design: We design a system that matches your home's requirements, including ductwork evaluation
  4. Equipment Selection: We recommend systems that match your needs, efficiency goals, and budget
  5. Written Proposal: Detailed quote including all equipment, installation, and warranty information
  6. Professional Installation: Our certified technicians install your system to manufacturer specifications
  7. Ductwork Services: Repair, modification, or replacement of ductwork as needed
  8. System Commissioning: Complete testing to verify proper operation
  9. Customer Training: We show you how to operate and maintain your new system

Why Proper Installation Matters

Studies show that improper installation can reduce HVAC efficiency by 30% or more. Common installation problems include:

  • Incorrect Sizing: Oversized or undersized systems waste energy and fail to maintain comfort
  • Improper Refrigerant Charge: Too much or too little refrigerant reduces efficiency and damages compressors
  • Ductwork Problems: Leaky or undersized ducts waste conditioned air
  • Airflow Issues: Poor airflow causes uneven temperatures and premature wear

Our certified technicians follow manufacturer specifications and industry best practices to ensure your installation performs as designed.
