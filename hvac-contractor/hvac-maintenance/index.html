HVAC Maintenance Plans in Lake Charles, LA

Preventive maintenance that extends equipment life, improves efficiency, and prevents costly breakdowns. Priority scheduling and repair discounts included.

An HVAC maintenance plan is the best way to protect your heating and cooling investment. Latour's HVAC maintenance plans include annual tune-ups for both your AC and heating system, plus priority scheduling, repair discounts, and peace of mind knowing your system is in top condition.

Louisiana's climate is tough on HVAC equipment. Your AC runs almost constantly from April through October, and humidity challenges your system year-round. Regular maintenance isn't a luxury—it's essential for reliable operation and reasonable energy bills.

Benefits of Regular HVAC Maintenance

  • Prevent Breakdowns: 80% of HVAC failures could be prevented with regular maintenance. Tune-ups catch small problems before they become big repairs.
  • Lower Energy Bills: A clean, tuned system operates 15-25% more efficiently than a neglected one.
  • Extend Equipment Life: Well-maintained systems last 15-20 years vs. 10-12 years for neglected equipment.
  • Maintain Warranty: Most manufacturer warranties require annual professional maintenance.
  • Better Air Quality: Clean coils and fresh filters mean cleaner air for your family.
  • Consistent Comfort: Properly maintained systems keep temperatures more even.

What We Check During Tune-Ups

Cooling Season (AC Tune-Up)

  • Clean condenser and evaporator coils
  • Check refrigerant levels and pressures
  • Inspect and tighten electrical connections
  • Test capacitors and contactors
  • Lubricate moving parts
  • Check thermostat calibration
  • Clear condensate drain
  • Replace or clean air filter
  • Test system operation and temperatures

Heating Season (Furnace/Heat Pump Tune-Up)

  • Inspect heat exchanger (gas furnaces)
  • Check burner operation and gas pressure
  • Test safety controls
  • Check defrost cycle (heat pumps)
  • Inspect electrical connections
  • Test thermostat operation
  • Check flue and venting
  • Replace or clean air filter
  • Test system operation

Why Maintenance Matters in Louisiana

Lake Charles' climate puts extra demands on HVAC systems:

  • Long Cooling Season: Your AC runs 7+ months per year
  • High Humidity: Systems work harder to remove moisture
  • Gulf Air: Salt and humidity accelerate corrosion
  • Storm Debris: Outdoor units need regular cleaning

Regular maintenance addresses these challenges and keeps your system performing its best.
