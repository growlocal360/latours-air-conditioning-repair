AC Installation in Lake Charles, LA

Professional air conditioning installation with proper sizing, quality equipment, and expert workmanship. Free in-home estimates and flexible financing available.

Investing in a new AC installation in Lake Charles is a major decision. A properly installed air conditioning system does more than cool your home—it controls humidity, improves air quality, and operates efficiently to keep energy costs manageable. In Louisiana's hot, humid climate, proper installation is crucial for comfort and longevity.

Our AC Installation Process

  1. Free In-Home Consultation: We evaluate your home's cooling needs, existing ductwork, and discuss your comfort goals and budget.
  2. Load Calculation: We perform a Manual J load calculation to determine the exact cooling capacity your home needs—no guessing.
  3. Equipment Selection: We recommend systems that match your needs, explaining efficiency ratings, features, and costs.
  4. Written Proposal: You receive a detailed quote with no hidden fees. We explain everything before you decide.
  5. Professional Installation: Our certified technicians install your system following manufacturer specifications and building codes.
  6. System Testing: We verify proper operation, refrigerant charge, and airflow before we leave.
  7. Customer Training: We show you how to operate your new system and thermostat.

Types of AC Systems We Install

  • Central Air Conditioning: Traditional split systems with an outdoor condenser and indoor air handler. Ideal for homes with existing ductwork.
  • Heat Pumps: Energy-efficient systems that provide both heating and cooling. Perfect for Louisiana's mild winters.
  • Ductless Mini Splits: Versatile systems for room additions, garages, or homes without ductwork.
  • Packaged Units: All-in-one systems for homes without space for separate indoor/outdoor units.

Why Proper Sizing Matters

Many homeowners don't realize that bigger isn't always better when it comes to air conditioning:

  • Oversized AC: Cools quickly but short-cycles, failing to remove humidity and causing uneven temperatures.
  • Undersized AC: Runs constantly, struggling to cool your home and wearing out prematurely.
  • Properly Sized AC: Runs longer cycles that effectively remove humidity, maintain even temperatures, and operate efficiently.

We perform detailed load calculations to ensure your new AC is perfectly sized for your home.
