Common AC Problems We Repair

Our NATE-certified technicians diagnose and repair all types of air conditioning problems:

  • AC Not Cooling: The most common complaint. Could be low refrigerant, dirty coils, compressor issues, or thermostat problems.
  • AC Not Turning On: Often caused by electrical issues, capacitor failure, or thermostat malfunctions.
  • AC Blowing Warm Air: Usually indicates refrigerant leaks, compressor problems, or airflow restrictions.
  • Strange Noises: Grinding, squealing, or banging sounds indicate mechanical problems that need immediate attention.
  • AC Short Cycling: Frequent on/off cycling often means an oversized unit, refrigerant issues, or electrical problems.
  • High Humidity: AC running but not removing humidity properly may need coil cleaning or refrigerant charge.
  • Water Leaks: Clogged drain lines, frozen coils, or condensate pump failures.
  • Ice on AC Unit: Low refrigerant, poor airflow, or dirty coils can cause ice buildup.

When to Repair vs. Replace Your AC

We believe in honest recommendations. Here's our general guidance:

  • Repair: If your AC is under 10 years old and the repair is less than 50% of replacement cost
  • Consider Replacement: If your AC is 10-15 years old and needs major repairs
  • Replace: If your AC is over 15 years old, uses R-22 refrigerant, or needs multiple expensive repairs

We'll always give you options and help you make the best decision for your situation and budget.
