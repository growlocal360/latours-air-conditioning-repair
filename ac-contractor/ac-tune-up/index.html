AC Tune-Up in Lake Charles, LA

Professional air conditioning maintenance to keep your system running efficiently, prevent costly breakdowns, and extend equipment life.

A professional AC tune-up is the best way to keep your air conditioning system running reliably through Louisiana's long, hot summers. Latour's HVAC provides comprehensive maintenance that improves efficiency, prevents breakdowns, and extends the life of your equipment.

What's Included in Our AC Tune-Up

Our comprehensive tune-up includes a thorough inspection and service of your entire cooling system:

  • Inspect and clean condenser coils - Dirty coils reduce efficiency and can cause overheating
  • Check refrigerant levels - Low refrigerant indicates a leak and reduces cooling capacity
  • Inspect and clean evaporator coil - Ensures proper heat transfer and humidity removal
  • Check and tighten electrical connections - Loose connections can cause failures and safety hazards
  • Test capacitors and contactors - These components are common failure points
  • Lubricate moving parts - Reduces friction and wear on motors and bearings
  • Check thermostat calibration - Ensures accurate temperature control
  • Inspect ductwork for leaks - Leaky ducts waste energy and reduce comfort
  • Replace or clean air filter - Dirty filters restrict airflow and reduce efficiency
  • Clear condensate drain - Clogged drains cause water damage and humidity problems
  • Check blower motor and fan - Ensures proper airflow throughout your home
  • Test system operation - Verify proper cooling and cycling

Benefits of Regular AC Maintenance

  • Prevent Breakdowns: Most AC failures are preventable with regular maintenance. A tune-up catches small problems before they become expensive repairs.
  • Lower Energy Bills: A well-maintained AC operates 15-25% more efficiently than a neglected one, saving money every month.
  • Extend Equipment Life: Regular maintenance can extend your AC's lifespan from 10-12 years to 15-20 years.
  • Maintain Warranty: Many manufacturers require annual maintenance to keep your warranty valid.
  • Better Comfort: A tuned-up AC maintains more consistent temperatures and better humidity control.
  • Improved Air Quality: Clean coils and fresh filters mean cleaner air for your family.

How Often Should You Get an AC Tune-Up?

In Louisiana's demanding climate, we recommend:

  • Spring: Schedule a tune-up in March or April before the cooling season begins
  • Fall (Optional): A second tune-up in October can catch issues from the summer season

At minimum, your AC should be professionally serviced once per year. Twice-yearly service is ideal for systems that run year-round.
