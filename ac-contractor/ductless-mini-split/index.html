Ductless Mini Split Installation in Lake Charles, LA

Energy-efficient zoned cooling and heating without the need for ductwork. Perfect for room additions, garages, converted spaces, and whole-home comfort.

Ductless mini split systems offer a flexible, energy-efficient solution for cooling and heating spaces where traditional ducted systems aren't practical. Latour's HVAC installs and services ductless systems from top brands, providing Lake Charles homeowners with comfortable, efficient climate control.

Whether you're adding a room, converting a garage, or want zone control throughout your home, a ductless mini split system may be the perfect solution. These versatile systems provide both cooling and heating with efficiency ratings that often exceed traditional HVAC systems.

How Ductless Mini Splits Work

A ductless mini split system consists of two main components:

  • Outdoor Unit: A compact condenser/compressor unit installed outside your home
  • Indoor Unit(s): Wall-mounted or ceiling-mounted air handlers installed in each zone

The two are connected by a small conduit containing refrigerant lines, power, and drain. No ductwork required—just a 3-inch hole through the wall.

Perfect Applications for Ductless Systems

  • Room Additions: Add cooling and heating without extending existing ductwork
  • Garage Conversions: Turn your garage into a comfortable living space
  • Sunrooms: Enjoy your sunroom year-round with efficient climate control
  • Home Offices: Keep your workspace comfortable without cooling the whole house
  • Above-Garage Apartments: Independent climate control for rental or guest spaces
  • Older Homes: Add AC to homes without existing ductwork
  • Hot/Cold Rooms: Supplement central AC in rooms that never seem comfortable
  • Multi-Zone Homes: Create separate comfort zones throughout your home

Benefits of Ductless Mini Splits

  • Energy Efficiency: SEER ratings up to 30+, far exceeding standard AC systems
  • Zoned Comfort: Control temperature independently in each room
  • No Duct Losses: Ducted systems can lose 25-30% of energy through leaky ductwork
  • Easy Installation: Minimal construction required—usually completed in one day
  • Quiet Operation: Whisper-quiet indoor units with sound levels as low as 19 dB
  • Heating and Cooling: Most units provide both, eliminating the need for separate systems
  • Improved Air Quality: Multi-stage filtration removes dust, allergens, and contaminants
  • Compact Design: Small outdoor units and sleek indoor units preserve your home's appearance

Single-Zone vs. Multi-Zone Systems

Single-Zone: One outdoor unit connects to one indoor unit. Ideal for cooling a single room or space.

Multi-Zone: One outdoor unit connects to multiple indoor units (up to 8). Each zone has independent temperature control. Perfect for whole-home comfort or multiple rooms.
