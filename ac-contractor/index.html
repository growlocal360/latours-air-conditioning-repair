Signs You Need an AC Contractor

Not sure if you need professional help? Here are common signs it's time to call an air conditioning contractor:

  • AC blowing warm air or not cooling effectively
  • Unusual noises like grinding, squealing, or banging
  • Frequent cycling on and off
  • High humidity levels inside your home
  • Unexplained increase in energy bills
  • Ice forming on the AC unit or refrigerant lines
  • Unpleasant odors from the vents
  • AC unit is 10+ years old and needs frequent repairs
