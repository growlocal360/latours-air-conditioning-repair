About DeRidder, LA

DeRidder is the parish seat of Beauregard Parish, located about 45 miles north of Lake Charles. With a population of around 11,000, DeRidder serves as a hub for the surrounding rural communities and is closely connected to Fort Polk military base. The city features historic architecture in its downtown area and is a gateway to the Kisatchie National Forest.
