Common HVAC Issues in Sulphur Homes

Our Sulphur service calls often address these common issues:

  • Frozen AC Coils: Often caused by low refrigerant or restricted airflow
  • High Humidity: Louisiana's humidity can overwhelm undersized or aging AC systems
  • Dirty Filters: Pet dander, pollen, and dust clog filters quickly in this area
  • Thermostat Problems: Outdated thermostats waste energy and reduce comfort
  • Duct Leaks: Common in older Sulphur homes, causing efficiency losses
